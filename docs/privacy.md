# TaxUpdates Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 28 May 2026

TaxUpdates is built and maintained as an independent, single-developer project. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data — the short version is **we don't collect any**.

## Data we do not collect

- We do not require or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.
- The app has no user accounts and no login.
- We operate no servers and do not transmit any of your data anywhere.

## What stays on your device

- All tax calculations (Income Tax, Capital Gains Tax, IR35 assessment) are performed entirely on your device.
- Information you enter into calculators — salary, gains, losses, IR35 answers — is held in the running app only and is not persisted, shared, or transmitted.
- Your acceptance of the in-app disclaimer is stored locally on your device using the system's standard local storage. It is not transmitted anywhere.

## Outbound requests

- The **Updates** tab fetches public tax-policy content directly from [gov.uk](https://www.gov.uk/) using their public Search API. The request is made from your device to gov.uk. We have no servers in the middle and no access to whatever logs gov.uk may keep of public API requests.
- When you tap a source link (in the Updates feed, on a deadline card, or in Settings → Sources), it opens that page in your device's browser. Once you are on the third-party site you are subject to their own privacy practices.

## Third-party services we link to

- **gov.uk** — UK government content. See [gov.uk privacy notice](https://www.gov.uk/help/privacy-notice).
- **Apple App Store** — handles app distribution. See [Apple's privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- **Expo Application Services (EAS)** — used to build and submit the app, not used at runtime. Does not receive any user data.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## Permissions the app requests

The app may request the following iOS permissions:

- **None at first launch.** No location, no contacts, no notifications, no camera, no microphone, no photo library.
- iOS may show standard system prompts if you use the **Export PDF** feature in the IR35 tool (e.g. to save to Files or share via AirDrop). Any sharing happens at the system level and is your choice — the app receives no information about what you do after the share sheet opens.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date above. The current version of this policy is always available at the URL where you are reading it now.
