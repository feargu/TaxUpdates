# TaxUpdates

An iOS app for UK tax practitioners. Calculators, an IR35 decision tool with PDF export, a live regulatory updates feed from gov.uk, and key deadlines — all on your phone.

## Built for

- Tax practitioners working on UK individual and small-business engagements
- Anyone who wants quick, defensible UK tax calculations on the go
- 2025/26 tax year coverage

## Features in v1

- **Calculators** — UK Income Tax (rUK bands, NIC, marginal rate, PA taper) and Capital Gains Tax (post-Oct 2024 rates, AEA, basic/higher band split)
- **IR35 Status Indicator** — weighted assessment against the foundational case-law factors, with HMRC source citations per question and PDF export via the iOS share sheet
- **Updates** — live feed of HMRC and HM Treasury content from gov.uk, refreshed on demand
- **Deadlines** — fixed UK tax deadlines with countdown and direct gov.uk source links

## Privacy

TaxUpdates collects no personal data, uses no analytics, and operates no servers. All calculations run on your device.
